FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?

Typically 1-2 days, but it will also depend on the size and complexity of the job. We guarantee a quote within 48 hours, and your project completed within 2 weeks of acceptance.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, we provide a manufacturer’s warranty on materials and a workmanship guarantee.

Can Colorbond fencing withstand harsh weather?

Colorbond fence is engineered to withstand harsh weather conditions, including rain, wind, and extreme temperatures. Its durability makes the best choice for all types of fencing requirements.

How do I maintain my Colorbond fence?

The best way to maintain your fence is by washing it gently with water. If there is some dirt buildup, you can also use soap. Don’t ever use a high-pressure washer or acidic cleaners.
